Certification Badges

Trust signals for quality certifications, compliance standards, and product attributes. Not for status indicators like "New" or "Limited Stock".

Usage & Purpose

Green Badges

Environmental certifications: Sustainable, Organic, Eco-Friendly, Grass-Fed. Soft mint green builds eco-trust.

Blue Badges

Marine/seafood certifications: MSC, ASC, Friend of the Sea, Dolphin Safe. Ocean blue reinforces quality standards.

Gray Badges

Standard compliance: ISO 22000, HACCP, FDA Approved. Neutral gray for regulatory credentials.

Specifications: 6px radius (subtle, less than buttons' 8px), 6px 12px padding, 12px font size. Professional B2B appearance.

Green (Sustainable/Organic)

Environmental certifications that build eco-conscious trust with buyers.

Sustainable Organic Eco-Friendly 
<span class="cert-badge cert-badge-green">
  <span class="cert-badge-icon"></span>
  Sustainable
</span>
const GreenBadge = ({ children, icon }) => (
  <span className="cert-badge cert-badge-green">
    {icon ? (
      <span className="cert-badge-icon">
        <img src={icon} alt="" />
      </span>
    ) : (
      <span className="cert-badge-icon"></span>
    )}
    {children}
  </span>
);

// Usage
<GreenBadge>Sustainable</GreenBadge>
<GreenBadge icon="/logos/organic.svg">Organic</GreenBadge>
